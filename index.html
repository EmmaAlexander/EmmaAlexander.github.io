Emma L. Alexander

Astrophysicist working within radio astronomy and polarimetry, with a current research focus on radio galaxies and machine learning. Member of the POSSUM, QUOCKA, and EMU collaborations. Experience in observing (e.g. ATCA), data reduction (e.g. CASA), and data analysis (e.g. Python), working with Square Kilometre Array pathfinder telescopes. Communicating science, particularly astronomy, through a variety of outreach (presentations, demonstrations) and media (television, podcasting, and radio).
